The Early Years.

I grew up in Ohio, spending a lot of time outdoors, exploring the wilderness, and helping out on our farmland. At an early age (around 5 or so) my brother and I became increasingly interested in electronics and mechanical things. Naturally for two boys, this meant taking apart nearly every toy we owned or acquired, just to see how it worked.

Schooling.

During my younger school years, I developed somewhat of passion for math and the sciences. I discovered I had many interests. Right out of high school, I spent two years at DeVry University studying Electrical Engineering & Computer Science and 4 years at Ohio University where I graduated with a B.S.C.S. in Computer Science, a 4 year bachelor degree.

Professional Work.

During most of my 20's, I worked at IT companies, dabbling in all aspects of computing and technologies. In my 30's I began getting more heavily into Computer Science and Programming, and began work in research and software development companies, mainly studying the C++ langauge along with its capabilities and uses.
